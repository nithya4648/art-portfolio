The Maker





Hello! I’m Nithyadharshini, an artist who finds peace and calm in the process of drawing. There's a sense of tranquility I experience when I put pencil to paper, allowing my thoughts to flow through each line and shade.


I love drawing people, capturing the emotions and details that make them unique. Beyond that, I also draw characters or elements that represent specific moments and situations in my life, each artwork telling its own story, reflecting a personal experience or feeling.


Art, for me, is more than just a passion—it's a way to express myself and share pieces of my journey with the world.





Journey



A Canvas of Beginnings: Where My Art Took Flight


The Spark of Creation


It all began as a whimsical adventure in grade 7. Back then, art was my playground—a space where curiosity and creativity danced together. My inspiration came from my friend’s father, a gifted artist whose works felt like windows into other worlds. Captivated by the beauty of his creations, I picked up a pencil and began to play, drawing and shading with no purpose other than joy. Those early days were like the dawn of a painting—full of light, experimentation, and wonder. Every stroke, every smudge of graphite, whispered a new possibility, and I fell in love with the magic of turning blank spaces into stories.


The Journey Through Time


As the years passed, life added more shades to my canvas. The lockdown—a time of quiet and reflection—became the turning point in my artistic journey. With endless hours at hand, I delved deeper, learning the art of pencil drawings and experimenting with methods that brought my imagination to life. The world outside may have paused, but within, my creativity flourished. From intricate portraits to abstract expressions, I explored every corner of the artistic spectrum. Art was no longer just a pastime—it became my language, my solace, my way of connecting with the world.



Whispers on the Canvas: A Journey Still Unfolding


Art has been my constant companion—a journey of growth, discovery, and endless creation. From playful sketches in childhood to the intricate portraits I create today, every stroke has been a step toward becoming the artist I aspire to be.Each drawing holds a piece of my soul, a fragment of the joy, curiosity, and determination that fuels my craft. As I continue to grow, I carry with me the essence of those early days—the love of creating, the thrill of exploring, and the beauty of discovering something new with every stroke.To anyone reading this, I hope my journey inspires you to embrace your passions, follow your instincts, and create fearlessly. After all, art is not just a skill—it’s a reflection of the world as seen through your heart.
